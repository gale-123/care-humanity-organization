About CHO

Care for Humanity Organization (CHO) is a humanitarian NGO founded by Simon Lominsuk Evans and registered in South Sudan. The organization works to eliminate poverty, hunger, and inequality through sustainable development programs and humanitarian assistance.

Vision

Dedicated to ending poverty and hunger in vulnerable communities.

Mission

Accelerating the elimination of poverty through strategic development initiatives.
